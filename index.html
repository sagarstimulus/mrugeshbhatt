Builders

‘Kyunki ghar bhavnaon se banta hai.’

Whenever we work on the ideation of real estate brands, we never want to create a brand that only showcases amenities and talks about its achievements. People have nothing to do with the awards you’ve won.

We always prefer to focus on their feelings, emotions, and everyday life problems. Like here, we tried to make people feel the nostalgia of childhood, when we used to write essays about ‘My Home’ during our school days.

In the second video, we prioritised the privacy that young people need.

For Aishwaryam, they wanted to showcase their project’s pre-construction site and help people understand the amenities and structure better. For that, we didn’t make regular promotional videos, nor did we simply share brochures or site plans. Instead, we organised a treasure hunt at the site itself. That way, people could explore and understand the project with genuine interest, and those moments and footages were later used to showcase the site on social media as well.

Television

‘Because food is not just food in India.’

We strongly believe that food is not just food in India. It’s culture. It’s a divine process. It’s emotion. It’s identity.

We got the opportunity to build the structure and elements of a television show in a way that allowed us to tell beautiful stories about different families.

Sports & Fitness

‘A shoe motivates you to run.’

When it comes to fitness or sports, you don’t need to motivate people by simply talking about sports or their benefits, people are already aware of that. Instead, we connected fitness with day-to-day life, everyday routines, and the small things people need for the activity.

While working in radio, we got the opportunity to work with mega brands like Adani Group sports initiatives and Pro Kabaddi League. We approached them differently.

While working with national brands like Cult.fit, we didn’t focus on workouts or gym equipment. Instead, we focused on the daily problems and hurdles that arise when you are not fit.

Sustainability

‘With a good cause, with a good message.’

When we got a free hand from our client, we decided to showcase our core strength…..storytelling. That’s where the magic happens.

We made a film where we connected the recycling process with the divine concept of reincarnation. And in the last sequence we metaphorically show the spinning wheel of a potter, a tire of cycle, the machinery rotations, the garba ground, everything is in circular momentum, a circle of life. A film that could connect with everyone. A story for all.

Healthcare

‘Because humans need warmth more than treatment.’

We got the opportunity to work with multi-speciality hospitals, where we realised that hospitals are obviously important, and well-equipped treatment is absolutely necessary, but something else matters just as much: emotion.

A family always cares deeply for the life of its loved ones. In the same way, we built campaigns and activities that helped create the image of a hospital that cares like a family member.
