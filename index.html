Builders

“kyunki ghar Bhavnaon se banta hein”

Whenever we are on the ideation of real estate brands, we never want to make a brand that only showcases their amenities and tells their success stories. People have nothing to do with the awards you won.

We always prefer to give attention to their feelings, their emotions, their daily life problems... Like here is a creative where we tried to make people feel that nostalgia of childhood when we used to write an essay about ‘My Home’.

Video: esha video maru ghar

In the second video, we prioritize the privacy that young people need.

Video: esha's video 2

For Aishwaryam, they wanted people to know about their pre-construction site and know more about the amenities and structure. For that, we didn't make any videos, we didn't share brochures or site plans. Instead, we arranged a game of ‘treasure hunt’ at their site. That is how people got to know about the site with interest, and those footages were used to showcase our site on social media as well.
